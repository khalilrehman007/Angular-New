Tilal Al Ghaf Villas
Overview.

A development by Majid Al Futtaim, Tilal Al Ghaf Residences will feature a unique blend of a modern and nature-inspired neighbourhood situated in the heart of Tilal Al Ghaf, Dubai. The upcoming community boasts sustainable initiatives through its development offering luxury villas, beach homes, and a stunning green environment.

Tilal Al Ghaf is a mixed-use development that will feature private residences, retail, and recreation centres as well as numerous exciting lifestyle offerings. To stay true to its initiatives, the location will also feature electric vehicles, bikes, as well as the use of solar-powered technology in order to create an environment-friendly community.

Aside from its modern design residences, the community will highlight an exceptional outdoor setup positioned in an ideal location perfect for all types of residents. This project will focus on providing its residents with a relaxing ambience and nature-driven neighbourhood featuring areas with water metering, recycling, and smart street lighting.

The community will be the home to spectacular family-friendly amenities including the Hive Park, an outdoor space with a lush green landscape ideal for family get-together and relaxation. Residents can also access the Hive Beach, a crystal clear lagoon lined with white sand suitable for any beach-related activities. There are also dedicated facilities for walking and cycling as well as an outdoor gym.

Furthermore, the community will be divided into four stunning neighbourhoods with each has distinct characteristics yet all will provide the ultimate living experience for its residents. Tilal Al Ghaf Residences is close to the Jumeirah Golf Estates and Dubai Studio City

A forward-thinking city deserves a forward-thinking community. A neighbourhood that puts mind, body, soul and the human connection at its heart, while enriching lives through a deep sense of belonging. A community that will grow with Dubai and is built on a tradition that understands that life, work, and play have one thing in common: you. Welcome to Tilal Al Ghaf.
